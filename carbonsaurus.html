Carbonsaurus

The growing climate crisis is affecting everyone globally but is not talked about enough. Thus, Carbonsaurus is a way for people to stay aware of climate change and understand their own carbon emissions on a day-to-day basis.

In Carbonsaurus, your virtual pet dinosaur will help you log your daily carbon emissions in a diary, browse the news and learn more about climate change through the use of quizzes and useful breakdowns.

However, your dino really doesn't like it when you use a lot of carbon, and is very prone to mood swings! You must keep your dino happy through accumulating dino points, a currency which you can then use to customize your dinosaur, including accessories and backgrounds.

Have fun and stay climate aware!
